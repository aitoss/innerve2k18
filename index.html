What is INNERVE 2k18 ?

Innerve 2k18 Hackathon provides you the platform to showcase your ideas and technical skills at a recognized level. This is the perfect opportunity to get those brilliant ideas out of your amazing minds and onto the computer screens. Coming again this year Innerve brings you with more interesting and challenging problem statements than ever before. So get ready for Innerve 3.0 to exhilarate you with newer ideas,more rewards and a fun packed experience.

THEMES

SMART CITY

We are letting the participants to take an un-orthodox approach for fast-paced growth and submit a development plan for betterment of the cities. This will let the participants to understand their challenges and by engaging with experts to get insights and solutions for a smarter way of developing city.

BLOCKCHAIN

Block chain, is envisioned to revolutionize human society over the coming decades in ways yet unimagined. Block chain technology provides new infrastructure to build the next innovative applications beyond crypto currencies, driving profound, positive changes across business, communities and society.

AI / ML

AI is taking roots in our everyday lives in a very subtle way. Machine’s ability to “think” like a human is the core philosophy behind Artificial Intelligence. With the help of AI. Not only it helps in increasing organizational efficiency, but it also reduces the possibility of any critical error. AI helps to detect irregular patterns like payment fraud and alert the firm about any suspicious activity. 

AUTOMATION

Mission to help developers automate repetitive processes and to increase employee productivity. A world of devices connected by internet opens up opportunities for us that were previously unthinkable. Build reusable components.

SECURITY APPS 2.0

With the ever-growing cyber threat landscape due to sophistication in nature of cyber-attacks it is imperative to innovate and build next generation secure applications.Develop next generation security apps right from SCRATCH or based on the -CONTROL: A cryptographic Digital Control Platform. Participants will be provided access to -SDK and -ADAPTER with API with documentation which can help build security apps 2.0.

HEALTHCARE

Looking at Healthcare in a completely new way - focusing on earlier, pre-symptomatic disease detection and prevention, instead of late diagnosis - helping clinicians access more information and intervene sooner with targeted treatments so their patients can lead longer, fuller lives.
